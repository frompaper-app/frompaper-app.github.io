From Paper — Document Scanner & PDF

Overview

From Paper is a document scanning app built around privacy-by-design and data minimization, in accordance with Regulation (EU) 2016/679 (GDPR). Your documents are stored on your device and, if you are signed in to iCloud, synced through your own private iCloud account so they are available across your Apple devices — they are never sent to us or to any server we operate, and we cannot access them. The only data that leaves the App and reaches us is anonymous, aggregated usage analytics — and only if you leave analytics enabled. This policy explains exactly what that means. As the App evolves, new features may involve processing or collecting additional data; whenever that is the case, we will update this policy to describe what is collected, why, and your choices, before or at the time the feature becomes available.

Your Documents Stay Yours

All scans, imported photos, generated PDFs, and settings are stored on your device. If you are signed in to iCloud, From Paper also keeps them in sync through your own private iCloud account so they stay up to date across your Apple devices — see “iCloud Sync” below for exactly how this works.

We (the developer) operate no cloud storage, remote servers, or external databases for your content, and we have no access to your documents or their contents, filenames, or file paths — whether on your device or in your iCloud. There is no copy of your documents on any system controlled by us. If you delete the App, the local copies are removed from your device; any content already synced to iCloud remains in your iCloud account until you remove it from within the App or through your iCloud settings.

iCloud Sync

If you are signed in to iCloud on your device, From Paper uses Apple’s iCloud to keep your documents in sync across the Apple devices that use the same Apple ID. This is handled entirely by Apple’s frameworks:

  • Document and page information (such as document names, dates, page order, and image-processing settings) is stored in From Paper’s private CloudKit database within your iCloud account.
  • Your original page images are stored in From Paper’s private iCloud container (iCloud Drive storage dedicated to the App).

In both cases the data lives in your own iCloud account, tied to your Apple ID, in storage that is private to you. We do not operate these servers, and we have no ability to access, read, or retrieve this content. From Paper has no backend of its own.

This sync happens automatically when you are signed in to iCloud and iCloud Drive is enabled. If iCloud is unavailable or turned off, the App works normally and simply keeps everything local to your device. You manage iCloud for From Paper in the iOS Settings app (your Apple ID → iCloud), and the App shows the current sync status under Settings → iCloud Sync.

Because this content is stored in your personal iCloud account, Apple processes it as part of iCloud. Apple’s handling of iCloud data is governed by Apple’s Privacy Policy and the iCloud Terms and Conditions.

Anonymous Usage Analytics

To understand how the App is used and to improve it, From Paper sends anonymous, privacy-preserving usage analytics through TelemetryDeck, an EU-based, privacy-first analytics provider (TelemetryDeck GmbH, Germany; data stored in the EU). This is the only data the App transmits to us, and it is entirely separate from the iCloud sync described above.

This is optional. You can turn it off at any time in Settings → Privacy → “Share Anonymous Usage Data.” It is enabled by default; when disabled, the analytics component is never started and nothing is transmitted.

What is sent

  • Anonymous usage events describing that an action happened, never its content — for example: a scan was completed, a document was exported (with format such as PDF or JPEG), a page was saved (with non-identifying settings like page size, orientation, color mode), or an error occurred (operation name and error code). Item and page counts are reported only as coarse buckets (e.g. 1, 2–5, 6–20, 20+), never exact counts.
  • Basic technical context automatically attached by TelemetryDeck: device model, operating-system version, screen resolution, app version and build, and language/region/locale.
  • A non-reversible, double-hashed device identifier generated by TelemetryDeck to estimate unique-user counts. It cannot be reversed to identify you and is not shared across apps.

What is never sent

The anonymous analytics events never include:

  • No names, email addresses, phone numbers, or other personal information
  • No document contents, text, filenames, or file paths
  • No photos or scanned images
  • No precise location
  • No advertising identifier (IDFA) or cross-app tracking identifiers
  • No free-text input

Because the data is anonymous and low-cardinality (only enumerated values and bucketed counts), it cannot be used to identify or track you. No Apple App Tracking Transparency prompt is required, and no data is used for advertising.

Legal basis

To the extent any of this constitutes personal data under the GDPR, our legal basis is our legitimate interest (Article 6(1)(f)) in understanding and improving the App, balanced by the anonymized design and the one-tap opt-out. Analytics data is processed by TelemetryDeck GmbH as our processor and stored within the European Union.

In-App Purchases

From Paper currently offers a one-time in-app purchase to unlock unlimited exports, and may offer additional paid features or purchase options in the future. All in-app purchases are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store using Apple’s StoreKit. We do not operate any user accounts or payment servers, and we never receive your payment details. Your purchase entitlements are verified on-device against your Apple ID, and any usage tied to entitlements (such as the number of free exports you have used) is stored only on your device and never transmitted to us. The unlock supports Apple Family Sharing; where you choose to share it, that sharing is handled entirely by Apple and brings us no additional data about you or your family members. Apple’s handling of purchase data is governed by Apple’s Privacy Policy.

Device Permissions

From Paper requests access to your device’s camera and photo library solely to scan and import documents. These permissions are managed by iOS and can be revoked at any time in your device’s Settings. All captured images and generated PDFs are stored on your device and, if you use iCloud, synced through your own private iCloud account as described above; they are never uploaded to any server operated by us.

Sharing

When you use the share feature to export a document, it is shared directly through your device’s built-in sharing mechanism (AirDrop, Mail, Messages, etc.). We have no access to shared content.

Third-Party Services

From Paper relies on the following third-party services, all described above:

  • TelemetryDeck (TelemetryDeck GmbH, Germany) — anonymous usage analytics, EU-hosted, opt-out available in Settings. See TelemetryDeck’s privacy policy.
  • Apple App Store / StoreKit — processing of the in-app purchase. See Apple’s Privacy Policy.
  • Apple iCloud / CloudKit — optional cross-device sync of your documents through your own private iCloud account; used only when you are signed in to iCloud, and managed through your device’s iCloud settings. See Apple’s Privacy Policy.

The App integrates no advertising networks and no other analytics or tracking SDKs.

Data Retention

We retain anonymous analytics only in aggregate form for as long as it is useful for product improvement. As the data is anonymous, it cannot be linked back to you or individually deleted; you can stop all future collection at any time using the in-app toggle.

Documents you sync to iCloud are retained in your own iCloud account under your control. You can delete them from within the App or manage them through your device’s iCloud settings at any time; we hold no separate copy and play no part in their retention.

Children’s Privacy

From Paper does not knowingly collect personal data from anyone, including children. The anonymous analytics described above contain no personal information. The App complies with applicable child-protection regulations, including COPPA (US) and GDPR Article 8 (EU).

Your Rights

Under the GDPR you have rights of access, rectification, erasure, restriction, objection, and data portability. Because From Paper holds no personal data that identifies you — your documents stay on your device or in your own private iCloud account, which we cannot access, and analytics are anonymous — there is generally no personal data on which to act. You can object to analytics processing at any time simply by switching off “Share Anonymous Usage Data” in Settings. If you have any concern, contact us at the email below, and you may also lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority (in Poland, the President of the Personal Data Protection Office, UODO).
